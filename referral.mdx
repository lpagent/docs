# Referral Program

LP Agent offers a comprehensive referral program that rewards users for bringing new participants to the platform and for becoming active liquidity provision leaders.

## Commission Structure

### Referral Commissions

- **10% commission** on all fees generated by users you refer
- Earn ongoing passive income from your referred users' trading activity
- No limit on the number of users you can refer

### LP Leader Rewards

- **20% commission** for qualifying as an LP leader
- Higher reward tier for active community builders and top performers
- Recognition as a platform leader with additional benefits

## Payout Schedule

### Monthly Distribution

All referral commissions are distributed on the **1st of each month** according to the following rules:

- **Minimum payout threshold**: 0.1 SOL
- **Above threshold**: Automatic payout on the 1st of the month
- **Below threshold**: Amount carries over and accumulates for the next month

### Accumulation System

If your monthly earnings don't reach the 0.1 SOL minimum:

- Your earnings are **automatically carried forward**
- No commissions are lost - they accumulate until the threshold is met
- Once the cumulative amount reaches 0.1 SOL, you'll receive the full payout

## Getting Started

1. **Access your referral dashboard** in the LP Agent app
2. **Generate your unique referral link**
3. **Share with your network** through social media, content, or direct outreach
4. **Track your earnings** in real-time through the dashboard
