## What is Auto-Compound?

Auto-compound is a powerful feature that automatically reinvests your earned fees and rewards back into your liquidity position, maximizing your potential returns through the power of compound interest.

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Fee Collection">
    LP Agent monitors and collects trading fees from your position
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reward Harvesting">
    Automatically claims any available protocol rewards
  </Step>
  <Step title="Optimal Reinvestment">
    Reinvests collected fees and rewards at the optimal time
  </Step>
  <Step title="Position Optimization">
    Adjusts position parameters for maximum yield potential
  </Step>
</Steps>
