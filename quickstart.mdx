## Getting Started with LP Agent

Follow these steps to begin automating your liquidity management.

### Prerequisites

- A Solana wallet (Phantom, Solflare, etc.)
- SOL for transaction fees
- Tokens for providing liquidity

### Connect Your Wallet

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install a Wallet">
    Install a supported Solana wallet if you haven't already
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect to LP Agent">
    Click "Connect Wallet" in the top right corner
  </Step>
  <Step title="Approve Connection">
    Approve the connection request in your wallet
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Create Your First Strategy

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Pool">
    Choose from our supported Orca or Meteora liquidity pools
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Parameters">
    Configure your risk tolerance and investment goals
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deposit Funds">Add liquidity to your chosen pool</Step>
  <Step title="Activate Automation">
    Enable LP Agent's automated management
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Monitor Performance

Once your strategy is active, you can:

- Track real-time performance metrics
- View historical returns
- Monitor position changes
- Adjust parameters as needed
