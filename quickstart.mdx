## Getting Started

Follow these simple steps to start using LP Agent's AI-powered liquidity management.

### 1. Login and Wallet Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect with Privy">
    Click "Login" and choose your preferred authentication method: - Email -
    Google account - Discord account
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Wallet">
    A secure Privy-powered wallet will be automatically created for you: -
    Non-custodial wallet - Fully controlled by you - Optimized for DeFi
    operations
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Your wallet is secured by Privy's enterprise-grade security infrastructure and
  remains under your complete control.
</Note>

### 2. Fund Your Wallet

<Steps>
  <Step title="Transfer SOL">
    Send SOL to your new wallet address: - From an existing wallet - Through a
    centralized exchange - Using a direct fiat on-ramp
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Balance">
    Ensure your funds have arrived before proceeding to position creation
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Always double-check the wallet address when transferring funds. Start with a
  small test transaction if you're unsure.
</Warning>

### 3. Choose Your Risk Profile

Select a risk profile that matches your investment goals:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Low Risk" icon="shield-check">
    - Conservative strategies - Stable pair focus - Lower but consistent returns
    - Minimal IL exposure
  </Card>
  <Card title="Medium Risk" icon="balance-scale">
    - Balanced approach - Mixed pair selection - Moderate yield targets -
    Managed IL exposure
  </Card>
  <Card title="High Risk" icon="rocket">
    - Aggressive strategies - Volatile pair focus - Higher potential returns -
    Active IL management
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### 4. Deploy with AI Suggestions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review Suggestions">
    Our AI analyzes market conditions and suggests optimal positions based on
    your risk profile
  </Step>
  <Step title="One-Click Deploy">
    Deploy your chosen position with a single click - no complex setup required
  </Step>
</Steps>
